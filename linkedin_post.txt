🚀 Excited to share my latest project, StadiaIQ, built for the PromptWars Challenge 4 (Smart Stadiums & Tournament Operations)! ⚽🏟️

During the FIFA World Cup 2026, stadium logistics and fan experience will be more complex than ever. StadiaIQ is a GenAI-enabled platform designed to optimize matchdays for Fans, Volunteers, and Tournament Organizers.

Key Features:
🗺️ Congestion-Aware Wayfinding: Live interactive SVG stadium maps that dynamically recalculate routes for fans to avoid congested gates based on live telemetry.
💬 Matchday Copilot: A conversational multi-lingual AI assistant helping fans with transport departures, match schedules, and bag policies in any language.
🌿 EcoGoal Sustainability Tracker: Fans can log eco-friendly actions (transit, recycling) to accumulate points and track real-time stadium carbon offsets.
🚨 AI Incident Intake Console: Volunteers report issues (spills, maintenance) in any language. Gemini translates, categorizes, and schedules resolution checklists automatically.
🤖 Strategic Command Center: Evaluates live telemetry and active incidents, generating emergency strategic plans and loudspeaker announcement drafts for tournament directors.

Built with a high-fidelity obsidian dark-mode interface (WCAG AA accessible) using:
💻 React + Vite Frontend
⚙️ Node.js + Express Serverless API
🤖 Google Gemini 2.5 Flash
🔒 Helmet, Rate Limiters, & XSS Security Middleware
🧪 Comprehensive Vitest suite (69/69 passing tests)
